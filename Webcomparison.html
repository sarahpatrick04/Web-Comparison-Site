Interactive Websites

A Comparison Between the Good & The Awful

The Good

Lets Go Rally

Portfolio Site

Lets Go Rally

Lets Go Rally, is a small business which offers creative direction and help with branding and as they say digital things.
I felt that this website immediately caught my attention by its interesting graphics and interactive way of
seeing each different project thats displayed on their main portfolio website.

I felt the strengths for this site was its simplified graphics, muted colors, and the interactive display of votes for each of their projects.
A weakness would be that when I first loaded the site it took until the fifth time reloading for there graphics to actually work.
Without them, there were no way to click on a project or see a glimpse or shot of what the project might even look like.

Dabshots

Portfolio Site

Dabshots

This particular website was my most favorite in there choice of color and simplified icons and 2d illustrations.
I felt its strengths were in the use of magnifying over the main illustration on the main page, where it showed the colorful and friendly illustrations. I felt this
showed there dedication and good work ethic with these very detailed characters and story. I also liked the vertical scrolling as well as the simple menu icons
which when you hovered over would show a description. I felt it was very useful in putting all the content on the main page without having to direct you to too many different
parts of the site. The only weakness I felt about this website was that the headlines in the illustration looked faded and hard to read, and just by my first reaction when
seeing the website I would have thought those were links until noticing the very small menu that scrolled with the page in the top heading of the site.

The Awful

Cyclelux

A Online Shop for Bike Enthusiasts

Cyclelux.cc

This website Cyclelux, is a digital online shop. I found this to be a unsuccessful interactive site because although they have interactive
hover over links where it displays the price of each product, the graphics are too simplified and it looks like it took two minutes to do
and also the pricing tags are displayed on top of some images and some are displayed right next to each product.
In addition, I was disappointed to see that a website with so much vertical information and products would not add any interactive scrolling or
menu to display each image. The site also does not have any hovering links and is just a normal link that directs you to different parts of the website.
